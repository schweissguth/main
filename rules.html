How to Play

  • Each week, every player selects two drivers.
    • Only one of the two drivers may be ranked inside the Top 10 in points.
    • A player cannot select a driver already chosen by another player that week, for that group.
  • After the race, points are awarded based on how each selected driver finishes relative to all other picked drivers:
    • Highest‑finishing selected driver → 16 points
    • Lowest‑finishing selected driver → 1 point
  • Bonus points:
    • Finish highest within your group (A or B) → +1 point
    • Your driver wins the race → +2 points
  • Maximum possible weekly score:
    • 34 points (16 + 15 + 2 + 1)
  • After scoring is complete, everything resets for the next race:
    • Players are randomly placed into Group A or Group B.
  • A weekly pick order is created for each group based on last week’s performance:
    • Lowest‑scoring player picks first; highest‑scoring player picks last.
  • Players pick drivers in order until both selections are made.

How to Win

  • After all 36 regular‑season races, the player with the most total points is the Regular Season Champion.
  • During the 10‑race Chase, the player with the most Chase points is the Chase Champion.
  • Additional 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place awards may be given (TBD).

Rules

  • A player may not select a driver already picked by another player that week.
  • Players must select drivers in the published weekly pick order.
  • Pick‑order tiebreakers are determined by current standings, with the lower‑ranked player winning the tiebreaker.
  • Picks cannot be changed once the race has started.
  • If a player forgets to pick or delays the group, they will be assigned the minimum points for both drivers (1 and 2 points).
  • If a driver is missing from the list, contact an admin to have it added.
  • If mid‑week standings changes cause a player to have two Top‑10 drivers, they are not required to change their picks.
